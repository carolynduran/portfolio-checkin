University of Michigan Club Gymnastics

This is the official website of the University of Michigan Club Gymnastics team. We are a student-run club sports team under the Rec Sports Department at the University of Michigan and a registered member of the National Association of Intercollegiate Gymnastics Clubs (NAIGC).

2019-2020 Officers: Sarah Hill, Amber Hooks, Alexis Osmond, Wesley Pasikowski, and Rachel Socha

About Us

All female and male undergraduate and graduate students, regardless of ability level, are welcome to join at any point during the fall or winter semesters. We hold practices five days per week and use the men's and women's varsity team training centers.

Schedule

Members can choose to participate in sanctioned NAIGC meets, where team members travel to and compete at approximately 4-6 competitions per year all over the country. In past seasons, our gymnasts have traveled to Ohio, Illinois, California, Indiana, Texas, and a variety of other places. NAIGC Nationals in 2020 will be held in Providence, Rhode Island.
